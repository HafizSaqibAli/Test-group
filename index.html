Dr Hafiz Saqib Ali

Principal Investigator · Research Associate

I am a computational scientist with a broad background in computational chemistry, chemical biology, medicinal chemistry, and machine learning. My work focuses on developing data-driven approaches to the design, synthesis, and evaluation of next-generation molecules — currently at the University of Strathclyde and GSK, Stevenage.
